About

Who am I?

I'm a second year computer game development student at the University of Limerick, Ireland. I'm from County Mayo, however, and it was there I cultivated my love for programming. I'm always seeking new ways to broaden my programming knowledge and to, of course, challenge myself.

Programming languages I have experience in?

Java is the language I have the most experience in. A years experience, to be precise. It is the language I spent my first year of college learning, and as a result I have an array of projects and small demos to show for it.

This very portfolio is an example of my experience in HTML and CSS. I also have earned freeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design certificate which entails approx. 300 hours of work. You can verify this certificate here.
