About

Who am I?

I'm a second year Computer Game Development student at the University of Limerick, Ireland. I'm from County Mayo however, and it was there I cultivated my love for programming. I'm always seeking new ways to broaden my programming knowledge and to, of course, challenge myself.

Programming languages I have experience in?

Java is the language I have the most experience in. A years experience, to be precise. It is the language I spent my first year of college learning, and as a result I have an array of projects and small demos to show for it.

This very portfolio is an example of my experience in HTML and CSS. I also have earned freeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design certificate which entails approx. 300 hours of work. You can verify this certificate here.

Other relevant skills/achievements?

Team Work Skills

The majority of the projects I've undertaken in college have been group projects, particularly the programming projects. This has allowed me to develop essiential team work skills.

Leadership Skills

I've been a class rep for two years now, and that responsibility of being the line of communication between my fellow students and my lecturers has taught me a lot about leadership. I'm also part of the voluntary Student Leader initiative at UL which involves a peer leadership structure to help first years transition into their programme. As a second year student, I've been assigned a group of five first years from my programme to mentor and help them adapt. Similarly to being a class rep, being responsible for other people, particularly those younger than me, has improved my leadership skills.

Academic Achievements

I have received two letters of academic achievement from the President of UL for my exam results in first year for having a QCA/GPA above 3.6. In regards to my exam results, do feel free to get in contact with me if you wish to see my transcripts.
